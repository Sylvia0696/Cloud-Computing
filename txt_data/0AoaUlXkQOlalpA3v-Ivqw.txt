Dare I say the best Middle Eastern food I've tasted so far in NYC? Believe it. The place itself is small. There is limited space for seating, 12 people the...I moved to the lower east side couple months ago and couldn't be more thrilled to discover this little gem... I have tried the Arabic  shawarma, falafel,...Great hole in the wall find! Definitely recommend Zyara if you like Mediterranean food. 

The chicken shawarma is easily the best option on the menu!...
